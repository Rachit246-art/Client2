ABOUT US

Pioneering Eco-Friendly Devotion

For over a decade, Div Mangal Murtis has been redefining festive celebrations by blending profound devotion with environmental responsibility.

Every Ganpati idol we craft is a masterpiece born from 100% natural clay and adorned with vibrant, toxin-free colors. Our vision is simple yet powerful: to ensure that our deepest traditions nurture the earth rather than harm it. When you bring home our Bappa, you are not just celebrating a festival—you are participating in a movement to protect our planet for generations to come.

Handcrafted by skilled traditional artisans, each idol is designed for a safe, easy visarjan that dissolves seamlessly back into nature. Join our family of over 50,000 happy devotees who have chosen to celebrate responsibly, leaving behind nothing but blessings and a greener future.
